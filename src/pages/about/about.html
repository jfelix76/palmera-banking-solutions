About

Palmera Banking Solutions is a unique expert services company that provides actionable insight for financial institutions, bank technology companies, and non-bank organizations as it relates to anti-money laundering and OFAC compliance. Years of functional experience in banking and technology companies, equips Palmera consultants to extend practical and efficient recommendations to address your organization’s regulatory requirements.

Palmera’s vision is to provide comprehensive, practical and affordable solutions for organizations required to comply with anti-money laundering and OFAC regulations. This vision is accomplished by utilizing professionals that have extensive knowledge and successful application of AML and OFAC regulations.

Sarah Beth Whetzel CAMS, M.F.S.

Sarah Beth Whetzel is a CAMS certified consultant, holds a master’s degree in forensics and is the founder of Palmera Banking Solutions. Prior to her current role, she directed the BSA Practice for a national compliance consulting firm. Her expertise includes the management of AML, BSA, and OFAC regulations in the form of operational risk, AML system validations, risk assessments, AML look-backs and BSA/AML compliance audits.

With over 15 years of experience in compliance and risk management for financial institutions and technology companies, she has experience working with bank technology companies, non-bank companies as well as financial institutions of all risk profiles and sizes, ranging from asset size of $1B-$12B. In both auditing and operational roles, software has been at the core of her experience.

Prior to her banking career, she developed a reputation for being a fraud prevention expert having managed fraud and transaction monitoring programs for one of the world’s largest transaction processors.

Sarah Beth holds a Bachelor of Arts in Business from Taylor University and a Master of Science in Forensics (M.F.S.) from Stevenson University. She has been CAMS certified for over 10 years and was featured in the CAMS’ Member Spotlight in 2006. She started Palmera Banking Solutions in 2010 after seeing a need in the marketplace for specialized and affordable BSA/AML solutions.
